Embedded Finance & Banking-as-a-Service

This platform let you add financial features — bank accounts, cards, loans — directly into your product without becoming a bank yourself.

 Miden
Website: miden.africa
Primary Markets: Nigeria and expanding across Africa
Backed by: Y Combinator (2024)

What It Does - Miden is a Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS) platform that lets any company embed financial services — virtual accounts, cards, and compliance infrastructure — into their product. Backed by Y Combinator and led by a team of ex-bankers, Miden has already issued over 100,000 cards.
If you're building a super app, an HR platform that needs to pay salaries, or any product that benefits from giving users a "bank account inside your app," Miden provides the regulated infrastructure so you don't have to get your own banking licence.

Why this matters for junior devs: BaaS APIs represent the next frontier instead of integrating payments into your app, you become the financial layer for your users. The developer who understands how to build with these APIs is building the fintech products of 2027.
